Jarville-la-Malgrange (French pronunciation: [ʒaʁvil la malɡʁɑ̃ʒ]) is a commune in the Meurthe-et-Moselle department in north-eastern France.

The Château de la Malgrange was a ducal residence of the House of Lorraine here.
